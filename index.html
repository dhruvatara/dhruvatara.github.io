Network Development Project / Vishwakarma Institute of Information Technology
Aug 2018 – Jun 2019

Designed and developed a peer-to-peer network using Bluetooth Low Energy to help a group of people, e.g. teachers and students on a school outing, a group of friends hiking or at a concert, etc. stick together, in the absence of network connectivity. A phone is required for configuring the devices and the 'master' device in the network, that keeps track of all other devices and alerts its user when a particular device has gone out of range. This threshold is predefined, and distance is measured relatively using RSSI. Users can also press a panic button on the device to alert devices in proximity that the user needs assistance.

Microcontroller: ESP32

Languages: Embedded C, Java

My contribution: Design of BLE packet contents, Android application to configure bands, securing communication using MD5 hashing, interfacing with OLED screens for display

You can find the project here, and our final report here.

Android App Development Project / Smart India Hackathon 2018
Jan 2018 – Apr 2018

Lead a team of six to the Grand Finale of Smart India Hackathon's 2018 edition, which is a pan-India hackathon that encourages students to harness technology to solve problems various organizations as well as the Government of India face. We tackled the Ministry of Water Affairs' problem of dissipating information among people living in low-lying areas who would be affected by release of water from dams near them. We created an Android application that could be used by ministry officials to load information about when water would be released from gated dams, as well as rainfall information for ungated dams(where water release isn't controlled). This application was also available in Hindi, and users could subscribe to certain areas to receive information about releases there, and would also receive notifications based on their proximity to these dams. Users could also share this information on their social media, to raise awareness.

Languages: Java

My contribution: Designed and implemented the Firebase database for the application, the scheduler function for displaying push notifications on devices based on activity in the database, and integrated the Google Maps API to calculate proximity from user and display dams on the map. Additionally, ensured all app content was available in Hindi.
